About the Drive

The Entrepreneurship Drive 2025 is a week-long initiative to inspire and nurture aspiring entrepreneurs. Participants engaged in workshops, mentorship sessions, pitch competitions, and networking events to develop business ideas and startup skills.

Events That Took Place

Workshop: Business Fundamentals

A hands-on session covering business modeling, market research, and strategy.

Mentorship Sessions

One-on-one mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs and industry experts.

Pitch Competition

Participants presented their startup ideas to a panel of investors and mentors.

Networking & Collaboration

Opportunities to connect with peers, investors, and incubators for future growth.
